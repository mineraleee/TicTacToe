🕹️ About the Game

Tic Tac Toe, also known as Noughts and Crosses, is a classic two-player strategy game that's simple yet brilliant. It challenges your logic, planning, and prediction skills, and it's incredibly fun!

📜 Game Rules

  • The game is played on a 3×3 grid.
  • Player 1 uses X, and Player 2 uses O.
  • Players take turns placing their symbol in an empty cell.
  • The first to get three in a row(horizontally, vertically, or diagonally) wins!
  • If all 9 cells are filled with no winner, the game ends in a draw.

💡 Tips

Think ahead, block your opponent’s moves, and always look for the chance to strike. Don’t underestimate the power of the center cell!
